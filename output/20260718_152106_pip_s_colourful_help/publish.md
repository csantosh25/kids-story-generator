Join Pip the small squirrel on a sweet evening adventure! He finds his friend Lily the ladybug feeling sad. Pip has a bright idea: they can look for Lily's lost leaf together! Even if they don't find exactly what they're looking for, they discover something even more wonderful: the joy of helping a friend and sharing beautiful new finds. This cozy story teaches little ones about kindness, friendship, and how good it feels to make others smile. Perfect for a gentle read-aloud before bedtime! ❤️📖
