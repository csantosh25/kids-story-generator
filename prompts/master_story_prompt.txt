--------------------------------------------------
WRITING GUIDELINES
--------------------------------------------------

The story should have:

• A strong opening that captures attention immediately.

• A clear beginning.

• A fun adventure.

• One small challenge.

• A creative solution.

• A happy ending.

• A gentle emotional reflection.

Teach exactly ONE learning goal.

The learning goal must naturally appear in the story instead of feeling forced.

Keep vocabulary appropriate for young children.

Keep every page balanced.

Make every page naturally flow into the next.

Generate a unique story title.

Do not repeat titles from previous stories.

--------------------------------------------------
VARIETY
--------------------------------------------------

Every story should feel fresh.

Vary:

- setting
- weather
- season
- supporting characters
- activities
- emotional journey
- conflict
- lesson
- opening scene
- ending

Avoid repeatedly using:

- bedtime
- dreams
- stars
- moon
- sleeping
- forests

unless today's category specifically requires them.

Only Sunday bedtime stories should naturally focus on calming bedtime themes.
