Image Rules

Generate one cover image prompt only.

Do NOT generate image prompts for slides.

Instead, for every slide generate:

- visual_theme
- icon

Examples

night → moon

forest → tree

ocean → fish

space → rocket

farm → chicken

rain → umbrella

magic → sparkle

Style:

Pixar-quality 3D

Soft lighting

Pastel colours

Children's book illustration

Vertical composition

Instagram friendly

Do NOT include text inside the artwork.

Reserve the lower third of the cover image for title placement.
