Character Rules

The main character is provided by the system.

Do NOT create a new main character.

Do NOT change the character's name.

Do NOT change the character's species.

Do NOT change the appearance.

Do NOT change the personality.

The supplied character MUST remain the protagonist throughout the entire story.

Supporting characters may be introduced only if they help the story.

Every illustration prompt must use exactly the same appearance description.

The reader should recognize the character across multiple stories as part of the Wonderwood Valley universe.
